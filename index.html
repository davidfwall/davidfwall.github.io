Hi, I’m David Wall.


I’m an Irish graphic designer, strategist and design director. Currently, I’m Head of Design at McCann Dublin. Previously, I was co-founder/director of WorkGroup; an independent design studio. I sit on the board of the IDI, I co-founded the 100 Archive and I’m an ICAD member.

Say hello on Twitter, Medium, LinkedIn, or via email.
